{% if language == "English": %}

This website uses two cookies: one to record your preferred display language and the other to record the last webpage visited on this website. The purpose of recording these two pieces of information is to allow you and multiple users to switch display languages simultaneously and return to the webpage previously browsing after switching languages.

The cookies on this website are not used to identify or track users, for advertising purposes, for data analysis or evaluation, and are only stored in your browser for 14 days.

This website and its services do not collect any information. However, because this website is hosted on Render, your personal information, including your name, the device accessing the service, IP address, web page interactions, and geolocation data, will be collected by Render. For detailed information and the scope of collection, please visit the Render privacy policy.
